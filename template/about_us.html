This website and budget calculator was designed, coded, and published by Kelby Hubbard, Yali Wang, and Rose Peters. We are all Computer Science majors at the University of Alaska Fairbanks. This application was created for the group project requirement in CS 371 -- Computer Ethics and Techinical Communication for the Fall Semester of 2020. Suggestions and way to contact please head over to our Suggestions page.

Current budget calculator products, whether web applications or soft-ware, have similar basic functions such as calculation and displaying chart analysis. Some products have added suggestions modules, but many of them have complex page outlooks. Our products are dedicated to satisfying basic calculation and statistics, chart analysis, and suggestion modules. We have compared three products of the same type with our product. We found that our budget calculator product is more concise on pages, easier to use, and can better protect users’ privacy.

Our budget calculator is free for use, has simple and clean pages out-looks, has multiple functions, and can better protect users’ privacy. Our product can be used offline which helps reduce the user’s data traffic
expenses and provide convenience for users. Our idea is not to put the product on the server. We will let users download the product package and use it directly offline. Users can also choose to run the product on our
Github.
